After months of planning, the day finally arrived. We met at 7am, crammed the car full of bags, and hit the road.

## The Plan

Before leaving, we mapped out the full route — roughly **2,400 miles** over **7 days** through California.

The rough outline:
- San Francisco → coastal drive
- Big Sur → Hearst Castle
- Los Angeles → Joshua Tree
- Las Vegas (detour) → back home
