The trip starts here. We land at SFO early afternoon, grab the rental, and point east toward the Sierra Nevada. By nightfall we're sleeping under canvas in Yosemite Valley.

## Plan

- Land at SFO at around 1pm (hopefully)
- Pick up rental car and load luggage
- Drive to Yosemite Valley (~4 hrs)
- Optional detour: Columbia State Historic Park (Gold Rush town, see below)
- Check in at Curry Village tent for the night

## Optional Detour: Columbia State Historic Park

About halfway between Stockton and Yosemite, Columbia is a well-preserved Gold Rush town we can walk through for free. Worth a 45-minute stop if we're not in a rush. Skip if time is tight.

**Address:** 22708 Broadway St, Columbia, CA 95310

## Curry Village

Staying at [Curry Village](https://www.travelyosemite.com/lodging/half-dome-village/) (Half Dome Village) in canvas tent cabins. Central location in the valley — easy access to trails, shuttle stops, and the main visitor area.

<div class="tip">

**Tip:** The valley shuttle system is free and runs throughout Yosemite. Park the car when we arrive and rely on shuttles during our stay.

</div>
