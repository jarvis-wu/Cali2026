LA is a city you experience in fragments. Nobody walks anywhere, the freeway is a religion, and somehow it all works. We had one day — we made it count.

## Morning: Griffith Observatory

Parked at the Greek Theatre lot and hiked up. The view of the Hollywood sign and the city basin is as good as advertised. The observatory itself is free to enter — the planetarium shows cost a few dollars.

**Get there early.** Parking fills by 10am on weekends.

## Afternoon: The Obligatory In-N-Out Stop

We drove to the one on Sunset Blvd. The line was 45 minutes. Zero regrets. Order the Double-Double animal style.

## Evening: Santa Monica Pier

Ended the day watching the sun set over the Pacific from the pier. The Ferris wheel lit up as dusk came in. Classic.
