The valley is ours for the day. No destination, no rush — just work through a handful of classic hikes at an easy pace. We'll need the legs fresh tomorrow.

## Plan

- Bridalveil Fall (short, 0.5 mi — good warmup)
- Tunnel View / Artist Point (short walk, unbeatable panoramic view of the valley)
- Yosemite Falls Trail (lower falls out-and-back, ~2.6 mi)
- Mirror Lake Loop (flat 5 mi loop, quiet in the morning)
- Early dinner, early bed — Half Dome permit day starts at 4am

## Bridalveil Fall

A 620-foot ribbon of water visible from the road, but worth the short walk to the base. Gets you sprayed if the flow is strong. Takes 20 minutes and sets the tone for the day.

## Tunnel View

Pull off at the east end of the Wawona Tunnel for the postcard view: El Capitan on the left, Half Dome dead ahead, Bridalveil Fall on the right. This is the one we've all seen in photos. Artist Point is a short hike above if we want a higher angle.

## Yosemite Falls

The lower falls trail is a gentle 2.6-mile round trip ending at the base of North America's tallest waterfall. Nothing strenuous — keep the pace easy.

## Mirror Lake Loop

Flat 5-mile loop around the lake with reflections of Half Dome on calm mornings. Best done early before the wind picks up. This is the last hike of the day — after this we're done.

## Where We're Staying

Upgraded to a wooden cabin at Curry Village tonight — actual beds, actual walls. Worth it before a big day.
